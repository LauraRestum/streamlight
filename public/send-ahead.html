Prototyping & Development

Sample to Production.
Controlled and Fast.

Physical sample to approved production prototype in weeks, not months. Every stage documented, every revision controlled, and the transition to production happens in the same facility.

01

Sample Receipt & Engineering Review

Receive existing product, document dimensions, materials, construction, fit points, and retention geometry.

02

Domestic Material Sourcing & Quote Basis

Source domestic material equivalents, confirm construction approach, and define the cost basis for quoting.

03

Prototype Build & Customer Review

Build initial prototypes using approved materials and construction. Submit for customer review with full documentation.

04

Production Transition

Lock cost basis, finalize routing, and move directly into production within the same facility. No handoff delay.
